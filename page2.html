Moon Keder


At Kedermaker, it is in our very nature to innovate and create to benefit our loyal customers, therefore we are glad to introduce a new range of our most recent patented creations.

Moon Keder- a semi-circular keder designed and developed by Kedermaker to provide a new generation of keder.

Their functions are exactly the same as an ordinary keder, but more economical and lighter in weight (up to 30%); the reduced area allows for a smoother insert into keder profiles, and the lighter weight is most ideal for roof loading on large tents and membrane structures where this lighter weight could make all the difference.

Moon Keder can only be achieved with our specialized Full-Bonding Adhesion technology.
